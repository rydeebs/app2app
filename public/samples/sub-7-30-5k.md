# Sub-7:30 5K Plan

**Goal:** Run a 5K at a 7:30/mile pace (sub-23:18 total) within 8 weeks.
**Current:** 5K at about 8:15/mile pace (~25:40 total).

## Weekly structure
- **3 runs/week:** one easy run, one interval/speed session, one long run.
- Rest or cross-train on the other days.
- Track every run's distance and pace; the pace is what matters.

## Workouts
- **Easy run:** 2–3 miles at a conversational pace. Keep it relaxed.
- **Intervals:** warm up 1 mile, then 4–6 × 400m at goal pace (1:52 per 400m) with 90s jog recovery, cool down 1 mile.
- **Long run:** start at 4 miles, add ~0.5 mile each week, up to 6–7 miles. Easy pace.

## Progression
- Weeks 1–2: build the routine, easy efforts, establish a baseline 5K time.
- Weeks 3–5: add a tempo mile at goal pace to the easy run.
- Weeks 6–7: full goal-pace intervals; one time-trial 5K in week 7.
- Week 8: taper, then race the 5K.

## Reminders
- Don't skip the easy runs — they build the aerobic base.
- Hydrate the day before a hard session.
- Log every run so progress toward 7:30 pace is visible.
